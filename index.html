How to get the correct CAS PDF
1. Go to camsonline.com or kfintech.com
2. Statement type → Detailed
3. Period → Since Inception  ·  Folios → With zero balance folios
4. Enter registered email + create a PDF password → submit → PDF arrives by email
⚠️ Do NOT upload a CDSL or NSDL CAS — those are demat statements and are not supported.
Only CAMS/KFintech MF statements work. If your PDF has a CDSL or NSDL logo, request a fresh one from CAMS/KFintech.
